Hey! I'm Sawyer.

project manager / technical lead / developer at Counterpart

Written by Sawyer Powell - 2023-12-05 Tue 17:34

There is an immense joy in creating systems which are truly useful. Computing offers us a platform to extend our own capabilities, but leveraging its power requires creativity, discipline, and an appreciation of good design. In my work I hope to express and explore this beauty, and to pay respect to the incredible intelligence of the people who got us here. In every line of code, in every flash of pixels on our screens, is the life's work of hundreds of thousands of people. It's vast, and stunning. Use this blog to explore my essays and projects. A good amount of my technical notes are also available.
